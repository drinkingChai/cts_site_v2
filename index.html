  • E-Rate

E-Rate is a federally funded program offered through USAC (Universal Service Administrative Company) and it reimburses costs related to telecommunications, Internet Access and internal connections on eligible products and services required by schools and libraries.

Charter Technology Solutions is a registered service provider under the USAC program and its SPIN (Service Provider Identification Number) is 143035558.

Once selected via the competitive bidding process, we work closely with the Operations team and/or E-Rate consultants of public, private or charter schools. In addition to the technology plans you develop, the Form 471 and Item 21 attachments ensure maximum funding for your technology needs.
